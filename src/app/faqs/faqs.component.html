FAQs

What is GamePoint?

GamePoint is a platform that connects video gamers, allowing them to play their favorite games for cash! We are redefining the online video gaming experience, providing video gamers and newcomers alike a safe and legal place to engage in competitive matches.

How do I sign up?

Just go to our home page and hit “Sign Up”. Complete the steps that follow and you are all done.

Is there a sign-up fee?

No! Signing up with GamePoint is absolutely free.

What video games and consoles are supported on GamePoint?

Currently, we support FIFA 18,19,20,21,22 and PES 18,19,20,21,22 on 2 console series: XBOX and PS (PlayStation)

Is it legal to earn money through GamePoint?

Yes! GamePoint is a skill-based earning platform that allows you to monetize your video-gaming skills. Skill-based gaming has a well-established legal, social and commercial history. Games supported by GamePoint take the clear distinction as being games of skill.

How do I play a match?

Refer to the how it works section on our homepage. Write to us here: xx if you have any doubts.

How do I report results?

  • Once a match is finished players can enter results of the match on the website by uploading the result screenshot of the match.
  • Both users have to agree to results
  • Once results are agreed, funds will be credited to the winners account.
  • In the case that you or your opponent don’t agree to a result a dispute will be created

How are disputes resolved?

We will map a dispute against a user’s account and after 10 disputes are mapped for a user, they will be banned from playing for 3 months

What are the minimum and maximum amount that I can bid for a match?

The minimum entry fee is Rs. 100 and maximum are Rs. 1000. The entry fee can be multiples of 50.

How much will I get if I win a match?

If you bet Rs. 150 and the opponent bets Rs. 150, the game pool becomes 300. If you win the match, you will get the game pool after a deduction of 20% which is the matching fee that GamePoint will charge. In this case, you will get Rs. 240 (after deduction of Rs. 60 as the matching fee from the gaming pool).

Can I have more than one account?

GamePoint users are limited to a single account only. Xbox Gamertags and PSN IDs are unique and cannot be reused in other accounts. There might be some rare cases that we will allow a user to create a separate account, pending our discretion.
(e.g., a user lost access to a prior email address and registered for an account using a new email address, in which case the user may be restricted to one username and account, the additional account will be deleted).
